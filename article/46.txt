A bright spot for me turned out to be reading. My love of the written word began early as my mother read to me every evening. D. I loved the colorful photographs in the books. I quickly graduated from typical children's books to ones with fewer pictures and longer chapters. Reading opened new worlds to me. A. I wasn't alone any longer.
